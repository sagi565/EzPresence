## **4. Data Storage and Security**

We take your privacy and data security seriously.  
- Data retrieved from social media APIs is **stored securely on enterprise-grade cloud infrastructure** and used solely to provide analytics, scheduling, and creative services.  
- Content you create or upload to EZpresence is **stored within your account** so you can manage, edit, schedule, and publish it. You may delete this content at any time; deleted content may remain in encrypted backups for up to **7 days** before being permanently erased.  
- Aggregated, non-identifiable data may be retained to improve system performance and AI models.  
- Access to data is restricted to authorized personnel only and protected using industry-standard encryption and security practices.
